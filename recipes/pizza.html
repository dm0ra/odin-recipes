Pizza

Description

My grandfather always used to make incredible pizza for us every sunday morning. My siblings and I's mouths would water every sunday after church waiting for his famous pizza.

If you're interested in tasting my this delicious pizza, look no further and i'll show you how to recreate this blessing.

Ingredients

  • An appetite
  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • sausage

Steps

  1. Lay bread on table
  2. Place cheese generously on bread
  3. Place cut sausage on cheese
  4. Place masterpiece in oven @ 450 F for 20 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
