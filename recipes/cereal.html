cereal

Description

My grandfather always used to make incredible cereal for us every sunday morning. My siblings and I's mouths would water every sunday after church waiting for his famous cereal.

If you're interested in tasting my this delicious cereal, look no further and i'll show you how to recreate this blessing.

Ingredients

  • An appetite
  • Milk
  • Cereal of choice
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Fill bowl with cereal. note: very important to place cereal into bowl before milk, otherwise you could be seen as a serial killer.
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Enjoy with spoon!
