PB&J

Description

My grandfather always used to make incredible PB&J for us every sunday morning. My siblings and I's mouths would water every sunday after church waiting for his famous PB&J.

If you're interested in tasting my this delicious PB&J, look no further and i'll show you how to recreate this blessing.

Ingredients

  • An appetite
  • Jelly of your choice
  • Bread
  • Peanut butter of choice

Steps

  1. Lay bread on table
  2. Place peanut butter on bread
  3. Place jelly on bread
  4. Place final piece of bread on top
  5. Enjoy!
